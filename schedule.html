SCUBA Classes with Thallahasse SCUBA

There are two options for taking a class with Thallahasse SCUBA through Life Worth Leading, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Regardless of your preference, please complete the online registration form. The registration form has all the pricing information. Note that we offer military and first responder discounts, scholarships for people with disabilities, and more.

1. Join a Scheduled Group Class

Group classes include textbook, workbook, log book, dive table, carrying case, instruction, pool sessions, 5 checkout dives (including 4 SCUBA dives and one skin dive), certification card, gear, and more.

Bring a dive buddy and you will each get $15 off! To secure a slot in a group class, please complete the online registration form. You can also take the Enriched Air Nitrox course as an add-on to Open Water.

2. Request a Private Class
