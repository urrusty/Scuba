Dr. Gabrielle K. Gabrielli

Course Director & Regional Manager

Dr. Gabrielle K. Gabrielli has been an avid SCUBA diver her entire adult life and earned instructor certifications with four different agencies. She admits her "SCUBAddiction" and has logged 4,400+ dives in Florida and abroad in places like Galápagos, Fiji, Africa, Maldives, Philippines, Belize, Honduras, Bahamas, Costa Rica, and Mexico. Gabrielle enjoys diving in springs and in salt water. She earned her instructor certifications with SCUBA Educators International - SEI (formerly YMCA), HSA (Handicapped SCUBA Association), CMAS (Confédération Mondiale des Activités Subaquatiques), and Divers Alert Network instructor. She teaches many life-saving classes including CPR, First Aid, Oxygen Administration, and Neurological Assessment. Gabrielle has many other certifications including rescue diver, divemaster, advanced nitrox, scientific diver, and cavern. Gabrielle's passion for SCUBA is shared with her passion for education

She has a Ph.D. in education and her specialties are motivation, performance, and learning, so she loves to help people overcome any fear to conquer challenges. She likes to make the learning process fun, too! In her professional career, she is a keynote speaker, leadership coach, trainer, consultant, and evaluator.
