Skyler Morris

Divemaster in Training

Skyler Morris is a United States Marine Corps veteran who currently works as a sales representative for GE Healthcare. He was born and raised in South Georgia where he currently lives with his wife Emily, their beautiful 8-year-old daughter Hazel, and their Springer Spaniel named Sprig. Skyler loves all things to do with the water from fishing to diving, and he has a true passion for the outdoors. He started his SCUBA diving journey in 2024, and in 2025, he earned the Richard S. Gabrielle Leadership Scholarship.

The scholarship honoring Life Worth Leading’s founder Gabrielle K. Gabrielli’s godfather and uncle Richard S. Gabrielle provides Skyler with all the training toward his divemaster certification. Skyler says that the relaxing but thrilling nature of the sport has proven to be one of his favorite hobbies and has allowed him to meet many wonderful people. His passion for diving continues to grow with every underwater adventure.
